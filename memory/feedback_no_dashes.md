---
name: No em dashes
description: Never use em dashes (—) in copy or design, it looks AI-generated
type: feedback
---

Do not use em dashes (—) or en dashes (–) in any text content, website copy, or design elements.

**Why:** The user associates dashes with AI-generated text. It immediately makes the content feel inauthentic.

**How to apply:** When writing copy, restructure sentences to avoid dashes entirely. Use periods, commas, or separate sentences instead.
