THE EXPERIENCE

The first Alpine Reset

Three days in the Austrian Alps. Here is what it felt like.

MAY 2026

Twelve women, one mountain refuge above the Montafon, and three days with no agenda but to slow down. Our first Alpine Reset is over, and it was everything we hoped it would be.

This is a look back at what those days held, and a glimpse of what is waiting for you at the next one.

A DAY IN THE ALPS

How the weekend unfolded

Arrival and the first evening

We arrived as the afternoon light softened over the valley. A welcome drink, a goodie bag, a slow first dinner of glowing bowls, and the kind of conversations that start easily when nobody is in a hurry. By nightfall, strangers already felt like friends.

Move and reset

Saturday was the heart of it. A gentle morning activation, a long hike into the mountains, and time to simply breathe in the air up here. We came back for a sunset BBQ, gathered around the fire for a meditation, and let the day settle with journaling under the stars.

Glow and goodbye

The last morning began slow, with stretching and fascia work, then a Pilates session and a nourishing brunch. We closed in a circle, said what we were taking home with us, and left lighter than we arrived.
