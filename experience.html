THE EXPERIENCE

The first Alpine Reset

Three days in the Austrian Alps. Here is what it felt like.

MAY 2026

Twelve women, one mountain refuge above the Montafon, and three days we planned down to the last detail. From the first lavender matcha latte to the closing Pilates session, every moment was made with intention, and we left just as much room to breathe in between.

Here is how the weekend unfolded, and a glimpse of what is waiting for you at the next one. Everything, start to finish, fully gluten-free.

FRIDAY

Arrival and welcome

We welcomed everyone with a lavender matcha latte and homemade kefir, a little goodie bag to settle in, and a welcome ceremony with Leonie to set the tone for the days ahead. The first evening came together over a gluten-free glow bowl dinner and the kind of conversations that make strangers feel like old friends.

SATURDAY

Move, nourish, reset

Saturday started with a morning activation and gua sha with Eli, then a slow, nourishing gluten-free breakfast. From there we headed into the mountains for a long hike, with a relaxed lunch break in nature and plenty of time to take in the views. In the evening we grilled together, gathered around the fire for a meditation under the stars, and let the day soften.

SUNDAY

Glow and goodbye

The last day began grounded, with a fascia stretch led by Leonie and a relaxed gluten-free goodbye brunch. A longevity guest speaker shared how to feel good for the long run, and we ended strong with a Pilates sculpt session. Before everyone left, we gathered for the photo at the top of this page, lighter than we arrived.

ROOM TO BREATHE

Time to do nothing at all

We planned a lot, and we planned the pauses too. Between sessions there was always space to rest in the meadow, wander a little, or simply sit with the view. Those quiet moments mattered as much as everything on the schedule.
