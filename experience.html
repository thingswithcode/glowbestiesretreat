THE EXPERIENCE

The first Alpine Reset

Three days in the Austrian Alps. Here is what it felt like.

MAY 2026

Twelve women, one mountain refuge above the Montafon, and three days we planned down to the last detail. From the first lavender matcha latte to the closing Pilates session, every moment was made with intention, and we left just as much room to breathe in between.

Here is the whole weekend, moment by moment, and a glimpse of what is waiting for you at the next one. Everything, start to finish, fully gluten-free.

ROOM TO BREATHE

Time to do nothing at all

We planned a lot, and we planned the pauses too. Between sessions there was always space to rest in the meadow, wander a little, or simply sit with the view. Those quiet moments mattered as much as everything on the schedule.

Tanafreida. Above the Montafon.

A mountain refuge at over 1,000m, surrounded by nature, fresh mountain air and silence. Eight individually designed apartments, 40,000m² of meadows and forests, and views that make everything else fade away.
