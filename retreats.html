ABOUT THIS RETREAT

A few days designed to help you slow down, move your body, and reconnect with yourself - without losing your structure or ambition.

More details
THE EXPERIENCE

What you'll experience

Slow Mornings

Calm routines, sunlight, and space to ease into your day. Start with stretching, fresh air, and grounding rituals.

Intentional Movement

Fascia, mobility, Pilates, hiking, and light running. Movement that challenges and restores at the same time.

Nervous System Reset

Breathwork and guided sessions to shift out of stress mode. Learn tools you can take home with you.

Nourishment

Fully gluten-free meals focused on balance, energy, and quality. Prepared with care and enjoyed together.

Reflection & Clarity

Journaling and guided prompts to clear your mind. Create space for the thoughts that matter.

Real Connection

A small group, honest conversations, and shared experience. No small talk, just real moments.

NOURISHMENT

Fully gluten-free, without compromise.

All meals are fully gluten-free - focused on balance, quality, and enjoyment.

Tanafreida. Above the Montafon.

A mountain refuge at over 1,000m, surrounded by nature, meadows, and silence.
